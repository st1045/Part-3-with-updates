Our Story

Founded in 2012, MGB Logistics began as a small courier service in Johannesburg, South Africa. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted logistics company providing nationwide and cross-border delivery, freight transport, warehousing, and supply chain solutions. With over a decade of experience, we are dedicated to connecting businesses and communities through fast, reliable, and secure deliveries.

Our Mission

To deliver goods swiftly, safely, and sustainably, while providing outstanding customer service that builds long-term partnerships with clients.

Our Vision

To become Southern Africa’s most reliable logistics partner, known for innovation, efficiency, and a customer-first approach.

Why Choose Us?

✔ Over 10 years of logistics expertise.
✔ Nationwide coverage with international partnerships.
✔ Modern fleet of trucks, air freight, and warehousing facilities.
✔ Professional team dedicated to customer satisfaction.
✔ Technology-driven solutions including online tracking and booking.
